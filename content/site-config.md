## About SKG JS

We're a community of JavaScript enthusiasts in Thessaloniki, Greece. Founded in 2022, we organize monthly meetups covering everything from beginner-friendly intros to advanced deep dives.

**Our Mission**: Foster a welcoming, inclusive community where developers of all skill levels can learn, share, and grow together.

**What We Do**:
- Monthly technical talks and workshops
- Networking events
- Code challenges and hackathons
- Community support and mentorship

Everyone is welcome, regardless of experience level. Come for the talks, stay for the community!
